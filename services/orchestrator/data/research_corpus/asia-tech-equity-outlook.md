# Asia Tech Equity Outlook

Regional technology allocations remain sensitive to global AI infrastructure demand, semiconductor capex cycles, and US policy signaling. Investors should differentiate between durable earnings beneficiaries and high-beta names that are merely sentiment proxies.

In concentrated growth portfolios, valuation discipline matters as much as narrative momentum. When earnings revisions flatten, portfolios with excessive single-theme exposure may underperform despite a still-positive sector outlook.

For long-horizon investors, staged accumulation and explicit risk budgets remain more robust than chasing momentum after large rerating moves.
