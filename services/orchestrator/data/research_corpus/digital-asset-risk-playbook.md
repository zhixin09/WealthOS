# Digital Asset Risk Playbook

Concentrated crypto exposure increases drawdown risk because correlations across major digital assets often rise sharply during stress events. Investors with meaningful allocations to Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Solana should monitor portfolio concentration, liquidity needs, and the potential need to rebalance after outsized rallies.

Risk committees typically separate strategic digital asset exposure from speculative satellite positions. This makes it easier to cap downside, enforce position sizing, and avoid funding short-term liabilities with volatile crypto holdings.

Where custody, regulation, or exchange counterparty risk is uncertain, investors should prefer simpler portfolio structures and explicitly define what would trigger a partial de-risking decision.
