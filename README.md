# WealthOS

WealthOS is a Wealth Wellness Hub for relationship managers. Investors often hold wealth across deposits, public markets, property, private assets, and digital wallets, making it difficult to see their true financial health or react quickly when markets move. WealthOS brings these fragmented holdings into a single adviser view and turns them into actionable intelligence.

The platform combines a unified client dashboard with deterministic wellness analytics, including net worth, liquidity runway, debt burden, diversification, concentration risk, and digital asset exposure. It also links market events to portfolio holdings, calculates client-specific impact, and generates adviser-ready briefs and copilot recommendations backed by house-view research.

This helps relationship managers move from reactive servicing to proactive advice. Instead of reading generic headlines or manually piecing together client exposure, they can immediately see who is affected, how severe the risk is, and what action to take next. In short, WealthOS transforms fragmented wealth data into a clear, explainable, and actionable financial wellness workflow.
